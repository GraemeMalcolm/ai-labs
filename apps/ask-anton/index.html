🎓 About Ask Anton

Ask Anton is a sample AI agent designed to help you understand artificial intelligence concepts and topics.

📋 Important details and AI transparency notes (opens in new window)

Features

  • CPU-Only Local Model: Uses wllama with Phi-3-mini-4k-instruct-Q2_K in your browser
  • Grounded Responses: Answers are based on a curated knowledge base about AI topics
  • Voice Input: Use your microphone to ask questions naturally

How It Works

Ask Anton runs AI models entirely in your browser, so your questions remain private. No data is sent to external servers. The model runs locally on CPU for broad compatibility.

Getting Started

Try asking a question like "What is machine learning?" or "Explain artificial intelligence" to get started.
